About Me

CJ has been a technology enthusiast for many years. He has often wondered what a career in Software Development might look like. He completed an Immersive Full Stack Software Developer course with Codeworks. Immediately after completion, he began working as a Full-Stack Software Developer for In Time Tec. He went on to forge meaningful relationships and construct web application solutions. He worked on a Web Application for the state of Idaho called IdahoSTARS, which connected child-care facilities to requirements for accreditation and possible grants. The team cleaned up legacy code, constructed new features, and worked in all aspects of the code base from HTML, CSS, AngularJS, JavaScript, C#, .NET, EF Core, and MS SQL. Working on this project has been an eye opening and amazing opportunity. CJ has been thoroughly pleased that he made the change to the tech industry. He is very enthused about working with technology in teams of Developers. Building Apps is his new passion in life.
